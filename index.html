About Me

I am here to help you through your Life journey & through your tough days. I am Coach Mariam Magdi, Certified Parenting & Wellbeing Coach from Intellect - International Coaching Federation. Positive Discipline Educator from The Positive Discipline Association. Certified DIR \\ Floortime 101 from The International Council of Development & Learning. I am a mom to a lovely boy, Wife, and Wonder Woman who has gone through severe challenges in my life. These same challenges were the trigger to stand up and challenge back my life through understanding myself, changing my life, and changing others' lives as well. I have been working as a homeroom teacher in several schools and nurseries, dealing with kids & different parenting styles which inspired me to dig more trying to help them, moreover, dealing with my own child helped me through the process to understand myself as a parent and to understand the Parenthood Journey more. I am an expert in guiding parents through their parenthood journey and helping them to deal with the very unique character of each child. I Have been happily working with hundreds of Clients coaching them how to assess, analyze and resolve their live challenges using their skills and resources.
